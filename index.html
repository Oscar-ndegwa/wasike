About Us

Our Mission

At Oscar's Orphanage, we are dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and loving environment for children who have lost their parents or have been abandoned. Our mission is to give every child the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive care including:

  • Safe and comfortable housing
  • Quality education and tutoring
  • Nutritious meals three times a day
  • Healthcare and medical attention
  • Emotional support and counseling
  • Recreational activities and sports

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers

You can sponsor a child by visiting our Contribute section or contacting us directly. Sponsorship includes providing for a child's education, food, shelter, and healthcare. We'll provide you with regular updates about the child you sponsor.

Yes! We welcome visitors during our operating hours (Monday-Friday, 8AM-5PM, Saturday 9AM-2PM). Please contact us in advance to schedule your visit so we can prepare accordingly and ensure a meaningful experience for you and our children.

We accept clothing, books, school supplies, toys, sports equipment, and non-perishable food items. All donations should be new or in good condition. Please contact us before bringing large donations to ensure we have storage space available.

We're always looking for dedicated volunteers! Fill out our volunteer form below or contact us directly. We need help with tutoring, mentoring, maintenance, event planning, and various other activities. All volunteers must complete a background check.

Yes, Oscar's Orphanage is a registered non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible. We will provide you with a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.

100% of donations go directly toward caring for our children. This includes food, clothing, education, healthcare, facility maintenance, and staff salaries. We publish annual reports showing exactly how funds are allocated.
